About Me

My name is Arshin Soltan and I am a 21 year old Biomedical Engineering student from Tehran, Iran. During the last two years, I became very interested in coding and programming. When I was introduced to SheCodes, I finally found one of my biggest passions which was becoming a Front-end Developer; it was where design and creativity met technology and that was where I knew I had a lot to show. I have always been very enthusiastic to learn and grow my knowledge about my surroundings, and technology has always had a significant role in them. I love working with unfamiliar new challenges and I'm always open to new ideas to implement in everything I'm skillful in. I'm currently working on my web development skills and completing all my SheCodes workshops; and for my university major, I'm working on projects related to Artificial Intelligence and Biomedical Signal Processing.
